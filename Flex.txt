Flex is similar to Row or column, depending on direct: Axis.X. it is a flexible row or column.

      body: Flex(
        direction: Axis.vertical,
        children: [
          Expanded(
            flex: 1,
            child: Container(height: 10, color: Colors.red,)
          ),
          Expanded(
            flex: 2,
            child: Container(height: 10, color: Colors.blue,)),
          Expanded(
            flex: 1,
            child: Container(height: 10, color: Colors.green,)),
        ],
      ),
