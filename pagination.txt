Pagination in Flutter is the technique of loading data in small chunks (pages) instead of loading everything at once.

Pagination is a technique that splits large datasets into smaller, discrete "pages" of content (e.g., showing 20 items at a time instead of 1,000 at once). This improves performance, reduces memory usage, and creates a better user experience.

It is commonly used when fetching data from:
    APIs
    databases
    social media feeds
    chat apps
    product lists
    infinite scrolling apps


--- Core Flutter Widgets/Common Tools Commonly used for pagination: ---
    ListView.builder
    ScrollController
    FutureBuilder
    packages like; infinite_scroll_pagination
